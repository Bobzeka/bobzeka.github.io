Austin Robert Leal

Austin Leal is a second year student in computer science at the Georgia Institute of Technology with threads in Theory and Systems & Architecture. He is also pursing a certificate in the Spanish language. He has interests in high performance computing and advanced algorithms.

Austin has experience in developing Android applications through working on multiple team-based projects and web developement through an internship at Cisco Systems Inc. He is well-versed in Java, C, Matlab, HTML, and CSS.

Austin has been involved in the Mobile Application Development (GITMAD), Web Developement (GT WebDev), and Design clubs at Georgia Tech.

Outside of school, Austin enjoys participating in multi-cultural events and learning to play the piano.
